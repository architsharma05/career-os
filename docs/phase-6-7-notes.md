# CareerOS Phase 6 and 7 Notes

## Phase 6: Analytics and Polish

Implemented analytics should focus on job-search momentum rather than vanity metrics:

- Tracked jobs and active applications
- Interview and offer counts
- Average persisted AI match score
- Response rate from submitted applications
- Applications created in the last seven days
- Pipeline distribution by stage
- Reminder risk, especially overdue follow-ups
- Best matching jobs and top target companies

UI polish priorities:

- Keep tables horizontally scrollable on laptop-sized screens.
- Provide filter/search controls where lists can grow.
- Prefer explicit empty states that tell users what action to take next.
- Keep AI-generated content visibly review-only.

## Phase 7: Optional Future Enhancements

Phase 7 should remain ethical and user-approved. The safe connector architecture supports:

- RSS feeds that are publicly syndicated.
- Documented public APIs with credentials and rate limits.
- User-authorized email alert parsing.
- Manual save/browser-extension handoff flows initiated by the user.

Out of scope unless explicitly permitted by source terms:

- LinkedIn scraping.
- CAPTCHA solving.
- Automated job-board login.
- Mass applying without user review.
- Crawling pages that disallow it.
